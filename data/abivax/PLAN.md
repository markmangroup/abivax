# Abivax Operator Compendium — Roadmap

**Mission:** ERP go-live 1/1/27. VP, Lead of ERP.

**Principle:** Build what you use. Collapse branches that don't add value.

---

## Short-term (now → 4 weeks)

| Area | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **People** | Table: name, role, entity, need-them?, notes | You just started. Who's who, who matters. |
| **Timeline** | Table: key dates (go-live, offer expiry, milestones) | One place to see the critical path. |
| **Budget** | Table: SAP numbers, AACV, key financial constraints | Quick reference for numbers. |
| **Nav** | Sidebar: Spine \| People \| Timeline \| Budget \| Notes | Click through, find fast. |
| **Overview** | Spine State + Latest Digest on home | North star always visible. |

**Collapse if:** A section isn't opened for 2+ weeks → simplify or remove.

---

## Medium-term (1–3 months)

| Area | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Meetings** | Recurring meetings, who attends, purpose | Prep for the right people. |
| **Decisions log** | What, when, status | Track commitments. |
| **Risks** | Risk register, owner, mitigation | Board-grade narrative. |
| **Search** | Full-text across People, Notes, Budget | Find "P2P" or "Camille" fast. |
| **Tight bullets** | Enforce shorter summary bullets when pasting notes | Scanability. |

**Collapse if:** Decisions log becomes noise → fold into Notes. Risks unused → drop.

---

## Long-term (evolving)

| Area | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Links** | URLs to Confluence, SharePoint, Jira | One-click to source. |
| **Export** | Print/PDF for board prep | Operational readiness narrative. |
| **Light/dark** | Theme toggle | Preference. |
| **Integrations** | Calendar? Email? | Only if manual entry is the bottleneck. |

---

## Update cadence

- **PLAN.md:** Update when we add/remove/collapse a section.
- **Data files:** You paste notes → Cursor updates JSON. We add tables as you ask.
- **Review:** Every 4–6 weeks: what did you use? What didn't you touch? Prune.
