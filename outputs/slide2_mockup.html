ERP Program Overview

NetSuite selected February 26, 2026  ·  Implementation mobilization: April 2026  ·  Go-live: January 1, 2027

01
Business Rationale
Why this program exists
Current systems (Sage 100, legacy tools) are fragmented and highly manual across P2P, close, and reporting.
Controls environment is inconsistent and not adequate for SEC filing scrutiny or public-company audit standards.
US public market launch targeted December 2027. ERP go-live by January 2027 is a hard dependency.
SOX-readiness is the primary driver, not a technology swap
02
Selection Rationale
Why NetSuite was chosen
Multi-currency, multi-GAAP, bilingual (FR/EN) architecture built to scale with Abivax's France and US footprint.
Stronger controls environment from day one: segregation of duties, audit trail, and SOX-ready approval workflows.
Vendor-led delivery by NetSuite's own implementation team, ensuring product expertise and faster issue resolution.
KPMG led requirements definition and solution selection, confirming NetSuite as the best fit for Abivax's size and context.
KPMG-validated Selected Feb 26, 2026

Commercial and license negotiations began the week of Feb 26. Final package from NetSuite pending before Board readout.
