France P2P Current State

Current Flow We Need To Confirm

2 / 5

1. Intake and Approval

  • PO approvals start in DocuShare and AP receives an approval email after release.
  • The current approval matrix is driven by amount only.
  • Future-state routing should be tied to threshold plus department or cost center.

2. AP Processing

  • Juliette adds approved items into her master Excel payment tracker.
  • She checks invoices against the PO in the spreadsheet before booking in Sage.
  • If the invoice exceeds the PO, she reaches out manually and allows some leeway.

3. Payment Run

  • Only Juliette and Fatma can create vendors in Sage today.
  • Juliette enters bank information into Sage and Trustpair validates banking twice per day.
  • Payment execution still depends on manual checks and there is no formal reporting layer.
